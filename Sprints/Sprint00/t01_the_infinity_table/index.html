The Infinity Stones

Name Color Powers and capabilites Image
Soul Orange Allow the user to wield the Stone's power without suffering the normal repercussions.When Thanos successfully collected all of them, he used their phenomenal powers to annihilate half of all living creatures in the universe.
Time Green The Time Stone gives its owner complete control over the past, present and future. The wearer of the stone can foresee future events, travel to the past, influence the age of living beings. The Time Stone can be used as a weapon - enemies can be placed in infinite time loops. At its peak, and when used with other Infinity Stones, the Time Stone allows its wearer to exist in all time spans simultaneously.
Space Blue The Space Stone allows its owner to manipulate space at will. The main ability of the Stone is to move the owner to any desired location, regardless of distance. It can also increase the speed of the user. In addition, the stone allows you to appear in several places at once and change the distance between objects, contrary to the laws of physics. When combined with other Infinity Stones, it allows the wearer to be in all places in the universe.
Mind Yellow The Mind Stone grants its wearer psionic powers such as telepathy and telekinesis. The owner of the stone gains access to the thoughts and dreams of other beings. The Mind Stone can increase the user's mental abilities. When combined with other stones, the mind stone allows its wearer to connect with all consciousnesses in the universe at the same time. In addition, the mind stone is the embodiment of the subconscious of the universe.
Reality Red After the destruction and subsequent restoration of the Infinity Stones, the Reality Stone has changed. Now its carrier can observe other universes of the multiverse, as well as communicate with carriers of this stone from other realities.
Power Purple The Power Stone grants its owner all the power and energy that has ever existed, exists or will exist in the universe. The power stone also affects other infinity stones, for example, it can enhance their effects. This infinity stone makes its wearer stronger and grants them unlimited stamina. In addition, with the help of a power stone, its wearer can copy any superhuman ability. The power stone can also power inanimate objects.
